# Lumi

**Learn the words all around you.** Point your phone at anything — Lumi names it in the language you're learning, cuts it out as a sticker, and saves it as a flippable flashcard you can hear and review.

🌱 **Live at [lumiwords.com](https://lumiwords.com)**

## How it works

1. **Point & capture** — snap any object around you: a cup, a plant, a flower on the street.
2. **Named & cut out** — Lumi identifies it, names it in your language with a meaning and example sentence, and lifts it out of its background as a clean sticker.
3. **Collect & review** — every word becomes a card in your collection. Flip it between the word and its meaning, hear it spoken, and review over time.

Try your first 5 words free — no sign-up needed. Twelve languages, including the harder ones most apps skip (Kazakh, Korean, Japanese, and more).

## Built with

- Vanilla HTML / CSS / JavaScript — a fast, single-file mobile web app (no framework)
- Serverless functions on **Vercel** for object naming, translation, background removal, and text-to-speech
- **Supabase** for accounts and the saved collection
